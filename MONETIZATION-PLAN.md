# KiddosCompass Monetization Plan

## Phase 1: Now (pre-traffic)
- [x] Vote/recommendation system (built)
- [x] Parent Favorite badges (built)
- [ ] "Mention you found them on KiddosCompass" nudge on detail pages
- [ ] Buy domain (mykidscompass)

## Phase 2: When you have traffic (~500+ monthly visits)
- [ ] Tracked outbound links (/out/{id}) — log website clicks per business
- [ ] Monthly traffic report email to businesses — "We sent X parents to your site"
  - Subject: "KiddosCompass traffic to your listing"
  - Include: listing views, website clicks, phone taps
  - Soft pitch: "Want to appear at the top? We offer featured placements."
- [ ] Google Analytics or Plausible — track real pageview numbers

## Phase 3: When businesses notice you (~1000+ monthly visits)
- [ ] "Request Info" lead form on listings — inquiry email branded "New parent inquiry from KiddosCompass"
- [ ] Featured listings — $25-40/month to appear at top of category
- [ ] Sponsored placements — $60-120/month for homepage or category banner
- [ ] "Featured on KiddosCompass" badge businesses can put on their own website (drives backlinks)

## Phase 4: Real revenue
- [ ] Lead generation — businesses pay per lead ($10-25 per inquiry)
- [ ] Event promotion — event organizers pay $20-200 to feature events
- [ ] Affiliate partnerships — booking platforms, educational apps, camps

## Key Principle
Businesses pay when they SEE proof: traffic, calls, leads. Not just a listing.
The directory must act as a referral engine, not just a list.
